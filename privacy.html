Privacy Policy

Ova – Workout Timer · Last updated: [February 2026]

This privacy policy applies to the app Ova – Workout Timer and explains how we handle information when you use the app.

Data we collect

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your exercise and rest settings (e.g. durations and rounds) are stored only on your device and are not sent to our servers or any third party.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect:

  • Name, email, or contact information
  • Usage analytics or crash data (unless you use system features such as Apple’s crash reporting)
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers or tracking for ads

Third parties

The app does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or social networks. Sound playback uses the device’s built-in audio and respects your system settings (e.g. silent mode).

Children

The app does not target children and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when changes are made. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
